Digital Intervention in Handicraft and Handloom

The traditional handlooms and crafts, is the pride of Northeast Indian heritage, are now on the brink of extinction due to the decline of regular buyers, scarcity of raw material and the subsequent and increasing cost of production. For the preservance of unique heritage will be a valuable contribution towards saving our culture and identity but also a viable source of revenue for the rural artisans. Northeastern states of India consists of many varied nature ethicity and culture with their own way of Handicrafts and Handlooms. The artisans involved in this background are facing many challenging time with their crafts and arts, most parts of northeast is at vary remote from the commercial hub and urban consumers of northeast. Remote production is one factor that prevents the artisans from the know-how of demand in the market, and hence the decline. Digital Intervention in this sector will help them to incorporate them into todays compititive market scenario. Also, the lack of investments in this sector resulted in a non-existent supply chain creating bottlenecks in production.
